REGARDING: Stafford Lions Club 2015 Calendar

Staffordville Reservoir during Lions Club 2013 Moonlight Paddle

We are accepting images for the 2015 Stafford Lions Club Calendar. Come experience the beauty of Stafford, take photos and leave footprints!

What we need

  • Photos of Stafford (historic homes, buildings, parks, objects that are representative of Stafford, farms, open spaces, nature, waterways, conservation areas). Photo must be taken within the town limits of Stafford, CT.
  • Variety of Seasons (fall, winter, spring, summer)

How to present to us

  • Image in landscape orientation. Portrait orientation submissions will not be considered.
  • At least 3300 pixels by 2550 pixels & 300 dpi (bigger and more IS better!); images with poor resolution when enlarged will be rejected.
    Hint: use your camera’s largest file setting – you can always get rid of pixels, you can’t add!
  • .jpg or .tiff file format
  • Give us information:
    Who are you? Include full name and contact information.
    Where was photo taken? Verify it was taken within Stafford Town limits. Do you have a title? Let us know!
    Name of the file (please use format yournamePhotoTitle.jpg; example: jdoeMillPond.jpg)

Some Legal Stuff

  • If there are recognizable persons in your photo, please provide a release. If they are under 18, a parent must sign release.
  • You retain copyright of your photo. By entering a photo, you are agreeing that we may use in Stafford Lions Club calendar if selected.

Deadline for entry is August 1, 2014. Make submissions to StaffordCtLions@gmail.com with subject line “2015 Stafford Calendar submission.” Price of entry is $5 for first two images and $5 for each additional image (fees are tax deductible). Make checks out to “Stafford Lions Club” with a memo of your photo file name(s) and send to Stafford Lions Club, P.O. Box 143, Stafford Springs, CT 06076 (if your check is not received your photo will not be considered). If you have any questions, please call me at 860-684-3155.
